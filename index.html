FAQ

What is a hack day?

A hack day is a shorter version of a hackathon (which is usually 24+ hours). Participants design, program, and pitch a complete product to win prizes, hone their skills, and have a blast.

What do I need to know?

While we are only accepting high school and college students to participate, AlliumHacks welcomes participants of all skill levels to join us!

What do I need to bring?

Make sure you bring your laptop and its charger! Participants usually also like to bring items like headphones and pillows to get comfy while they work. On our end, we'll provide outlets, food, drinks, and Wi-Fi!

Who should I come with?

You can come by yourself or with friends. Most participants form teams, so you can also meet look for team members on the day of the event!
